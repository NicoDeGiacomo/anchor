# Grupo ACT - Phrases from Professional Resources

> Phrases extracted from images provided by Grupo ACT professionals. All phrases are in Spanish. Some use "vos" (Argentine Spanish), noted where applicable.

---

## Notes

- Images #4 and #5 are the same phrase in vos/tú forms: "No sos tus pensamientos" / "No eres tus pensamientos"
- Images #12 and #14 appear identical (same text, different visual style)
- Many phrases are ACT (Acceptance and Commitment Therapy) style — reflective questions and values-based prompts, not crisis intervention. These may work better as a new mode or in Grounding.
- The app currently uses "tú" form in Spanish content. Phrases using "vos" (#4, #9) would need adaptation for consistency.
